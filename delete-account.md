# Delete Your Account

You can delete your Hygge Activities account and all data associated with it at any time. Deletion is **immediate and permanent** — it cannot be undone.

## Option 1: In the app (recommended — instant)

1. Open **Hygge Activities**
2. Go to the **Profile** tab
3. Scroll down and tap **Delete Account**
4. Confirm

Your account and data are deleted immediately.

## Option 2: By email (if you no longer have the app)

Send an email to **hyggeactivites@gmail.com** from the email address your account is registered with, with the subject **"Account deletion request"**. We will delete your account and confirm to you within 30 days, as required by the GDPR.

## What gets deleted

Everything associated with your account:

- Your account and login credentials
- Your profile, photos, and all profile details
- All your messages and chats
- Activities you created, and your participation in others' activities
- Reviews, reports, blocks, likes, and settings
- Your business profile, if you have one
- Your push notification token

## Anything retained?

- Deleted data may persist in **encrypted database backups for up to 30 days** before being overwritten.
- Anonymous technical crash reports expire automatically after at most 90 days.

Nothing else is retained. For full details, see our [Privacy Policy](./privacy.html).
